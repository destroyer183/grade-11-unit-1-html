A Short Biography of Katherine Johnson

Origin/Debut

  • Started working at NASA in 1953
  • Is of African/American descent

Accomplishments

  • Performed the math/physics calculations for NASA's early space missions
  • When NASA started using "digital computers" she verified that they were working correctly
  • Arwarded the American Medal of Freedom by President Barack Obama in 2015
  • Had a movie made about her called Hidden Figures (2016)
