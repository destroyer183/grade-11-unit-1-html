A Short Biography of Shyra Barberstock

Information

  • Was adpoted as a yound child by a non-Indigenous family.
  • Taught herself computer software skills.
  • Became a teacher of computer software.
  • Met her birth mother at the age of 21 and discovered she is Anishinaabe.
  • In 2014, she launched Okwaho Network, an Indigenous-inspired social network for Canadian First Nations, Metis, and Inuit people, and the global Indigenous community, but open to all.
  • This social network is run by women.
